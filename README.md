🛍️ Customer Behaviour Analysis | End-to-End Data Analytics Project

📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow by analyzing customer shopping behavior using Python (Pandas), PostgreSQL, SQL, and Power BI. The objective was to clean raw retail data, answer real-world business questions, build an interactive dashboard, and generate actionable business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

🔍 Key Business Insights

Analyzed 3,900 customer records to understand purchasing behavior.
Clothing category generated the highest sales and revenue.
Male customers contributed significantly higher revenue than female customers.
Loyal customers represented the majority of the customer base.
Only 27% of customers had an active subscription.
Subscribers and non-subscribers showed nearly identical average spending.
Young Adult customers generated the highest sales and revenue.
Several products relied heavily on discounts to increase sales.
Express shipping customers spent slightly more than Standard shipping customers.

💡 Business Recommendations

Improve subscription benefits to increase customer adoption.
Increase marketing efforts to acquire new customers.
Launch personalized campaigns targeting female customers.
Reduce dependency on high discounts through pricing optimization.
Focus marketing and inventory on the Clothing category.
Promote highly rated products to improve customer satisfaction and repeat purchases.
